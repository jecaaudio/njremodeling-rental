About us

Helping people find a place to live and homes find a second life.

NJ Remodeling and Rental is based in Louisville, Kentucky, with a purpose that starts here and is built to grow across the country: helping people find affordable rentals that fit their needs, their budget, and the area where they want to live.

We believe finding a home should feel clear, respectful, and freeing. Our goal is to listen first, understand what each person is looking for, and help them move closer to a rental that feels practical, comfortable, and possible.

We also remodel and rebuild homes so they can have a second opportunity. Through repairs, upgrades, and a more modern look, we work to bring life back into properties while creating better spaces for families, renters, and owners.
